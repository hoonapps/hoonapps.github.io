# Code of Conduct

Hoonapps is a technical writing workspace. Discussion should stay useful, precise, and respectful.

## Standards

- Challenge claims with evidence.
- Assume technical work can be improved, including your own.
- Keep criticism specific to code, architecture, writing, or reasoning.
- Do not publish private information or credentials.
- Do not use harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Issues can be reported to `didgns10@gmail.com`. Reports are handled privately and judged by whether they protect the quality and safety of the project.
